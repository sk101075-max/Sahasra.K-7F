My Favourite Book (A Book Review)

Hello! So, first of all, I'm going to be ranking my top 3 books! Let's go.

  1. Daughter of the Siren Queen (Daughter of the Pirate King, 1st book)
  2. The Icebound Land (Ranger's Apprentice Book 3)
  3. Unravel Me (Shatter Me Book 2)

Now that I've told you about my top 3 books, let's talk about my favourite one,
Daughter of the Siren Queen.

Daughter of the Siren Queen follows Alosa, the daughter of the Pirate King, as she
races against her father to find the legendary Siren's treasure after a secret is
revealed that makes her question her loyalty. She is joined by her crew and the
first mate Riden, who complicates their journey as they evade her father's fleet
and Alosa grapples with her siren heritage.
